The Art Behind the Designs


Our wedding stationery features unique designs crafted from my own illustrations of native Australian birds and flora. Each piece showcases the vibrant beauty and intricate details of Australia’s natural wonders, reflecting your personal touch and love for these remarkable elements.

These hand-drawn illustrations are thoughtfully integrated into the design, bringing a distinctive and elegant charm to your invitations. The result is a beautifully crafted stationery collection that celebrates the natural beauty of Australia while perfectly complementing the high-quality paper.

Our Premium Paper Choice


Our wedding stationery is crafted from premium 350 gsm matte paper, chosen for its substantial feel and elegant appearance. This heavyweight paper ensures durability while maintaining a refined, high-quality touch that reflects the significance of your special day.

The paper's matte finish provides a smooth, non-glossy surface that enhances readability and allows for vibrant, true-to-life colors in your designs. Both the front and back of the stationery feature a silky, soft texture, adding a touch of sophistication and smoothness to the touch.

This high-quality paper and finish ensure that your wedding invitations will not only look stunning but also provide a tactile experience that enhances the anticipation of your special day.

Wedding Stationery Guide: Key Steps


  1. Send out save-the-dates to herald the wedding date and location.
  2. Arrange and issue elegant wedding invitations that comprehensively detail the ceremony and reception. Include additional information cards as needed, such as directions, accommodation options, and event schedules. Ensure RSVP cards are included to facilitate guest confirmations.
  3. Compile and manage the RSVPs guest list as responses come in.
  4. Utilize bespoke stationery on the wedding day, including finely crafted programs, menus, place cards, and favour tags.
  5. Send elegantly designed thank you cards after the wedding to convey your appreciation.
  6. Finalize and refine all stationery elements as the wedding day approaches.

For more information and suggested timeframes on sending out invitations please see our comprehensive Guide to Wedding Stationery
