FAQs

  • How far in advance should I order my wedding invitations?

    We recommend ordering your save the dates 8 to 10 months and your wedding invitations 6 to 8 months before your wedding date. This allows time for design, printing, and delivery. If you're on a tighter schedule, rush orders are available at an additional cost.

  • Can I customize my wedding invitations?

    Our invitations are semi-custom. You can choose from a range of preset designs, colors, and styles to match your wedding theme. While the designs and illustrations are set, you can customize the details, such as names, dates, and other event-specific information.

  • What is the cost of wedding invitations?

    The cost of wedding invitations varies depending on your needs and the size of your wedding. For a small, simple wedding, prices typically start around $500 for wedding invitations and an additional $500 for day-of stationery items, such as menus, programs, and place cards. For larger weddings, the cost of wedding invitations can range up to $1,500 or more, with day-of stationery and signage adding an extra $1,000 to $1,500. For a personalized quote tailored to your specific requirements, please contact us directly.

  • What is included in the wedding invitation package?

    Our standard wedding invitation package includes invitation cards, additional information cards (directions, accommodation options, event schedules), and RSVP cards.

  • How do I indicate dietary requirements for guests?

    Please include a section on your RSVP cards where guests can indicate any dietary requirements. This ensures we can accommodate all dietary needs when finalizing meal arrangements.

  • What is the typical turnaround time for invitations?

    The complete process, including design and delivery, typically takes 4 to 6 weeks. For a quicker turnaround, 2 to 3 week rush orders are available at an additional cost. Last-minute touch-ups or adjustments can also be accommodated for an extra fee.

  • Can I see a proof before finalizing my order?

    Yes, you will receive a digital proof of your invitations for review before we proceed with printing. This allows you to make any necessary changes to ensure everything is perfect.

  • What should I do if I need to make changes after placing my order?

    If you need to make changes after placing your order, please contact us as soon as possible. We will do our best to accommodate changes, but please note that additional fees may apply, especially for last-minute modifications.

  • How do I place a rush order?

    To place a rush order, simply let us know when you contact us to place your order. We will provide you with the details and additional costs associated with expedited processing and delivery.

  • Are international orders outside of Australia available?

    Yes, we accept international orders. For specific details on shipping times and costs to your location, please contact us.
