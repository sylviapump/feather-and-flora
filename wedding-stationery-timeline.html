Wedding Stationery Guide: Key Steps


  • 9 - 12 Months Before

    Determine your guest list and start collecting addresses. Send save-the-dates to announce the wedding date and location, giving guests ample time to make plans, especially for destination weddings or international guests.

  • 3 - 9 Months Before

    Send wedding invitations 6 to 9 months before the wedding for destination weddings or international guests, and 3 months before for all other guests. Include comprehensive details about the ceremony, reception, and any additional information such as directions and accommodation options. Ensure RSVP cards are included.

  • 1 - 2 Months Before

    Begin collecting RSVPs to create your seating chart and manage dietary requirements. Organise day-of stationery, including a welcome sign, programs, and menus. Ensure everything is prepared and ready for the wedding day.

  • 2 Months After

    Send thank you cards to all wedding guests with a personal note to express your appreciation. Include everyone who attended, even if they didnâ€™t bring a gift.
