Budget

The cost of wedding invitations varies based on your needs and the size of your wedding. For a small, simple event, you can expect to spend around $500 for invitations and an additional $500 for day-of stationery items like menus, programs, and place cards. For larger weddings, the cost can increase to $1,500 or more for invitations, with an additional $1,000 to $1,500 for day-of stationery.

Please provide an estimated budget for your order. This will help us tailor our services to your needs and ensure we offer options that fit within your budget.
