About Feather and Flora

Hello and welcome to Feather and Flora! I'm so thrilled you're here. My name is Sylvia, and I am the creator and designer behind Feather and Flora Wedding Stationery. Inspired by the incredible beauty of Australia’s landscape, I create wedding stationery that captures the essence of nature and celebrates your unique love story.

At Feather and Flora, each piece of stationery features my original hand-drawn illustrations of native Australian birds or flora. From the vibrant waratah and delicate wattle to the charming wrens and gorgeous robins, these designs are a tribute to the natural wonders of our homeland. I believe that these elements add a distinctive touch to your wedding, creating a meaningful connection to the beauty of nature.

I pour my heart into every detail, using only the finest materials to ensure your stationery is not just beautiful, but also of the highest quality. My goal is to create pieces that you and your guests will cherish, turning your wedding stationery into keepsakes that remind you of your special day.

Thank you for considering Feather and Flora for your wedding stationery. It’s a true honor to be a part of such an important moment in your life. I can’t wait to help you make your wedding day unforgettable with stationery that tells your unique story and reflects the natural beauty that inspires us all.

Feather and Flora is a LGBTIA+ friendly business.

We acknowledge Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples as the First Australians and Traditional Custodians of this land.
