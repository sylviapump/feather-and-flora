Order Process

  • Step 1: Explore and Choose

    Begin by exploring our curated collection of wedding stationery, featuring a range of exquisite artwork designs to enhance the elegance of your celebration. Once you've selected your ideal stationery and designs, please complete our contact form with your personal details, wedding information, and the specific items and designs you'd like to order. We will then provide you with a comprehensive quote tailored to your selections and event details.

  • Step 2: Quote and Deposit

    Upon approval of the quote, we will prepare an invoice and contract for your review. After confirming the invoice, you will be asked to make a 50% deposit and sign the contract to finalize your order. Once we receive your deposit, we will send you a form to collect additional details about your wedding.

  • Step 3: Design Mockup

    Next, we will create an initial digital design mockup for your review. You can provide feedback, request adjustments, or approve the design as presented. Once the final design and specifications are approved, we ask that you settle the remaining 50% of your invoice. After receiving full payment, we will begin printing your beautifully crafted stationery.

  • Step 4: Delivery

    When printing is complete, your elegant stationery will be shipped directly to you, along with an estimated delivery date.

Timeframe

Please allow 2-3 weeks for the design and printing of your wedding stationery, followed by an additional 2-3 weeks for delivery within Australia.
